Community Thrift Store, founded in 1982, is a non-profit organization that works with over 200 Bay Area charities. We accept donations on behalf of our charity partners, sell the items in our store, and then disburse the proceeds. Community Thrift Store is an independent and non-discriminatory establishment and when you donate to us you can pick your favorite charity! Whether you are looking to free up some space, donate to a vital community organization or find your next conversation piece, Community Thrift is the spot.

Community Thrift Store is certified as a San Francisco Green Business for meeting high environmental standards in the area, which includes our efforts in purchasing, use and disposal. San Francisco Green Businesses directly contribute to sustainability through pollution prevention, waste management, conservation practices, energy efficiency, and water and waste management. Through donations and shopping at Community Thrift, you are contributing to the city and the environment.Our Home. Our City. Our Planet.

At Community Thrift Store donors choose which charity organization will receive the proceeds from the sale of their donated items. When you make a purchase at CTS you will be supporting one of over 200 Bay area charity partners. Your purchase receipt will show which non-profit organization will benefit from the sale.
