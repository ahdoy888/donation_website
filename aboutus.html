We are Committed to Helping our Community

D&J Store relies on the generous donations of the local community, to collect used clothing and household items. We resell those items back to the community and share our profits with our charities, providing vital funding to help them improve lives affected by substance abuse, and assist children with illnesses.

Our Founders

D&J Store was founded in 2021 by Daniel and Jacky. They are both SF Bay Area residents, and they are proud and honored to help out there community as much as possible. Every year they plan on helping as many people as possible by providing them with there essential needs.
